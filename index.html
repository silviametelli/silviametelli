Bio.

I have joined the Personalised Healthcare Center of Excellence at Roche/Genentech in Basel. I am also part of the Turing-Roche Knowledge Share partnership. Before that, I was a Marie Skłodowska-Curie Individual Fellow at INSERM CRESS, and a postdoc at The Alan Turing Institute.

I am interested in novel statistical and Machine Learning methods for personalised health, digital health, dynamic networks and meta-analysis. I lead and support evidence generation strategies, end-to-end data pipelines and analytical workflows both for randomised and real world data studies.

More specifically, I work on:

  • Bayesian inference
  • prediction and survival modelling
  • time-series monitoring
  • comparative effectiveness research


Education.

MRes in Mathematical Sciences, Imperial College London
BSc, MSc in Statistical Sciences, University of Florence
